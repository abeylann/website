I'm a Software Engineer

As I mentioned above, I'm a backend developer at Deliveroo. I graduated from King's College London in 2018 with a BSc in Computer Science and Robotics.

What am I doing?

  • Ruby on Rails

    For the past 3 years I've been primarly a Ruby on Rails developer. I started as a student at Rails Girls London, becoming a mentor 6 months after and eventually part of the organising team.

  • Go

    As part of my new job, I've had to transition from Ruby to my beloved Go. Have less than one year of experience but really enjoying learning something new everyday and specially something so different to Ruby!

  • Hardware

    During my spare time, I also enjoy playing with different pieces of hardware such as Arduino and Raspberry Pi. I still have the dream of one day to build a Rosie .

  • Hackathons

    I've been attending hackathons constantly since 2015, eventually becoming an organiser of HackKing's and HackCortona and finally a EU coach for Major League Hacking.

  • Women in Tech

    I'm a huge advocate about women in tech and always working on making things better. To do this I'm a usual mentor for Rails Girls London and Code First Girls.
